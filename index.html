About Me

My name is Andrew, I am a pilot and have started to play around in programming and developing apps. I have been flying for several years, working my way up through the ranks to become a commercial pilot. I have a deep understanding of aviation and a commitment to safety. In addition to my flying experience, I also absolutely love building apps and trying to make things that solve problems.

I have completed several online courses and have a strong desire to learn more about the field. My main skills are HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and some Python. My background in aviation has given me a unique perspective on problem-solving and attention to detail, skills that I bring to my programming work.

I am excited about the opportunity to combine my two passions and use my knowledge of development to improve the aviation industry. I am dedicated to continuously learn and improve my skills in development and my passion for aviation will lead me to bring innovative solutions to enhance the field. I am eager to take on new challenges!
